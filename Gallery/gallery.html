Style: Hyperrealism

ABSTRACTION

A Breath of Summer II
by Schweinsberg, Germany (2019)

× A Breath of Summer II by Daniela Schweinsberg Medium: Acrylic, mixed media on canvas
Style: Abstraction
Germany, 2018 I consider nature to be a form of rest that offers balance in today's fast-paced everyday life and that runs through my works like a red thread. I question people's approach to nature and capture snapshots of their surroundings, which I transfer to canvas in abstract, dynamic, colorful lines and shapes. Daniela Schweinsberg

HYPERREALISM

An Angel At My Table
by Escofet, England (2018)

× An Angel At My Table by Miriam Escofet Medium: Oil on linen
Style: Hyperrealism
England, 2018 The portrait is of the my mother, who is an artist herself. My mother has a wonderful inner stillness and calm that I really wanted to convey in this work. She is at the centre of the pictorial space and the perspective of the tea crockery leads to a vanishing point contained within her. Miriam Escofet

CONTEMPORARY

Valley of Gold
by Muccillo, Canada (2020)

× Valley of Gold by Renato Muccillo Medium: Oil on panel
Style: Contemporary
Canada, 2020 My heart lies in the natural environment. I focus on the area where I live... and the experience of seeing what I see and being honest about it. I feel my best expressions are painted. I am continually driven to analyze and to try to understand the effects of light and atmosphere on color— then using my ability as an artist to decipher and record those effects. Renato Muccillo
